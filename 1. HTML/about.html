About Us

YM Clothing is a shop which offers anime clothing and it is under
the YM Store company which sells much more interesting things which are anime related,
such as home appliances, furniture, toys & more.

YM Clothing was established in 2016 by the owner of YM Store,
Yonela Mangele, when he received a lot of requests from the YM Store costumers
that loved buying anime related things and wished of wearing something with anime.

Our goal is to build a solid relationship with our customers by listening to their requests
and prividing them with specials that we believe are very affordable for anyone. With the
help of our customers, we will become bigger and compete with some of the best clothing stores.
